Hi, Jose Angelo, Let me give you some ideas about hosting in Airbnb.

Being an AIRBNB host is very financially rewarding and an enjoyable one wherein you meet people and eventually gain friends not just locally but globally. My experience made me feel like I am operating a hotel, wherein I take care of the upkeep of my properties and its essentials as well as attend to bookings and check in/check out of guests. Airbnb takes care of making my listings actively displayed in their platform so I do not worry about advertising it. I also benefit from the security of the payments made by guests. Lately, Airbnb even introduced the Aircover, which works as our insurance for any damage that may result in our bookings. That gives me confidence in renting out my condominium units. Let me know how I can help you get your place listed and welcome you to belong to the Airbnb family of hosts. Please continue listing your place, when you have a question feel free to ask me until we finish your listing. I will also give you tips on how to make your listing attractive to guests. As you continue listing your place, feel free to ask me your questions.

Imagine , no need to pay Airbnb in registering your property, its FREE. This is how it works, AIRBNB is an app wherein travelers looking for an accomodation can find a ¨homey¨place, because the properties are owned by private persons who registered as HOSTS, like you.

AIRBNB app will show your property based on location, specifics you described about your place, the rates and availability. You decide your rental rates AIRBNB will help you by making suggestions, but you are free to decide how much you are willing to rent your place. A HOST FEE about 15% of your published price, will be added by AIRBNB to your desired rate and the guest will see the total published rate, remember Airbnb fee is NOT deducted from your desired rate, it is added to the rate the guest will pay Airbnb. If it is acceptable to the guest, he will book instantly or request you to book, the APP will also as you to choose if you want guest to make a request or instantly book. Will tell you more…..

In summary, joining AIRBNB gives you a wider market to make your property be available to potential guests. This way, you save on marketing costs, technological support, since the APP offers you a complete system in communicating with your potential guests and actual guests. The system helps you display your property amenities, exact location, record of yous income as host, and 2 most important benefits as an AIRBNB host is protection of your income, the guest pays directly to AIRBNB via credit or debit card, so definitely you will be paid for every booking., you will give your bank details to Airbnb for them to deposit your fee. You do not need to issue your own Official Receipt since guests paid to AIrbnb and was issued a soft copy of receipt by AIRBNB. Another best feature is the AIRBNB Cover, it is an insurance covering the cost of damage caused by guests on your property, that is security for us hosts.

I hope you will start listing your place, ask me your questions along the way. Happy to help you anytime
