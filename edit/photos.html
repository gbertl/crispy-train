Manage photos

Every image is reviewed to make sure it meets experience photo requirements. We’ll email you to let you know when photos are live on your experience page.

Cover photo

Your cover photo represents your experience as a whole, and needs to include a person engaged in the main activity.

Gallery photos

Upload at least 4 more photos that highlight different aspects of your experience. Photos should include host/guest interaction, the location where you’re hosting, and close-up details of the experience.

Library

Manage all the photos you’ve uploaded.

Your photos must have

  • A variety of details and angles, including photos of people in action
  • Candid moments that accurately illustrate the experience
  • Good image quality—no heavy filters, distortions, overlaid text, or watermarks
