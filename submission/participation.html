Participation

We’re looking for engaging activities that are designed for “doing” and not just “watching.”

For example:
  • Follow-along projects that guests can make at home
  • Performances that include interaction with the audience
  • Questions, games, and interesting conversation

Guests should feel immersed in the experience the entire time, like they’re an active part of it.
