Upload your video

You can use any program you’d like to record your video.

A free Zoom account will allow you to record your screen—Zoom also offers a recording tutorial, if you need help. If you’re already an approved online experience host, you’ll need to use a personal Zoom account or a different program to record, since recording is disabled on Airbnb-provided Zoom accounts.

When you’re done, export the recording as an MP4 file. The file size must be under 1GB.
