Wu Qin Xi, Five Animal Plays Qigong
What we'll do:
We will meet online from the comfort of our home and we will have a unique path for learning one of the most classic Chinese ancient energy works, 五禽戏 Wu Qin Xi, 5 Animal Plays Qigong. Qigong is a system of coordinated body-posture and movement, breathing, and meditation used for the purposes of health, spirituality, and martial-arts training. With roots in Chinese medicine, philosophy, and martial arts, Qigong is traditionally viewed by the Chinese as a practice to cultivate and balance Qi (pronounced approximately as "chi" or "chee"), translated as "life energy". This is an interactive Experience great for a PRIVATE session but also wonderful for FAMILY members, couples, married couples, groups of FRIEND. In the end, you will receive an email with a pdf of our training as the textbook for review and practice by yourself to enjoy more. The Introduction of Wu Qin Xi: Wu Qin Xi is a kind of physical exercise in ancient China. It was founded by Huà Tuó (145 AD – 208 AD), a famous doctor in the late Eastern Han Dynasty (25 AD-220 AD). Its name and efficacy is recorded in the Biography of Hua Tuo in the Book of the Later Han Dynasty. He said he has an art called the Five Animals Plays: Tiger, Deer, Bear, Monkey and Bird. It is created to be a Health Qigong for taking away disease and balancing the body.

Relaxing Traditional Shadow Plays from Japan
What we'll do:
Shadow plays used to be one of very popular way for family members to enjoy themselves at home in old times in Japan. Sadly, they are getting less and less practiced in this modern days. This is a rare opportunity to experience Japanese traditional shagdow games with a Tokyo local. You will be invited to a "screen theater" at the host's home. In the darkness, she sets a light behind a traditional paper partition. She will show you many shadow shapes, from animals to daily life tools, behind the screen. Further, she will teach you how to make the shadows by yourself! 1. The host introduces herself and how she loved playing shadow games with her family when she was little. 2. She shows you a series of shadow shapes with a relaxing music behind. 3. The host reveals how she made each shape with her hands and body. 4. She teaches you the shadow shape making and helps you to become able to perform them. 5. The host answers questions from the guests. The experience will finish in about 1 hour - sometimes shorter or longer depending on how many questions and comments are made. Also, since the host needs to use a few devices and tools to show the performance, she might have to stop and take time to fix them if they got any minor issues. Join us and enjoy how shadows create beautiful and meditating experience, which can be performed by everyone from kids to grown-ups.
